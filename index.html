Our Club

Surrey's baseball
community
since 1992.

We've been building community around baseball in Guildford for over three decades — and we even built our own diamond at Kings College. Whether you've never held a baseball bat or you've played for years, you're welcome here.

From competing in the British Baseball Federation's Divisions 3 and 5 to running Saturday junior sessions for kids 14 and under — the Mavericks are the home of baseball in Surrey.

Kings College, Guildford

We built our
own diamond.

Most baseball clubs rely on borrowed pitches. We went further — the Mavericks raised funds and built a dedicated diamond at Kings College. A proper home ground with real bases, a pitcher's mound, and outfield grass.

Sunday fixtures. Thursday evening training. All of it right here in Guildford — no long drives, no pitch-sharing battles.
